Points of Interest

Arts and Music

Explore the dozens of museums and art galleries. If you are out at night, there can be live music found everywhere.

Shopping

Visit a market to find local handmade items or visit one of the many shopping districts to find top brands and designers.

Food and Drinks

Eat like a local by trying the local cuisine or street food while out in one the cities. You can also grab a local beer or drink.
